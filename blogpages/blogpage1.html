South Africa

Our next adventure brought as to South Africa. Our plan was to enjoy the modern capitol Capetown, visit the beautiful beaches of the Garden Route und of course to go on a Game Drive in the legendary Kruger National Park.

During our visit in the Kruger National Park we managed to see a lot of different animals thanks to our eagle eyed guide Trevor. We found Lions, Rhinos and even a Chameleon crossing the street.
